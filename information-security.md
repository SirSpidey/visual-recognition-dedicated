### Deleting data
{: #gdpr-visrec-ded-delete}

Models trained by a custom classifier generated with the {{site.data.keyword.visualrecognitionfull}} Dedicated service are not considered Personally Identifiable Information subject to GDPR protection.

If you need to remove processed images that may have been stored within the system to expedite subsequent training of your custom classifiers, create a support ticket for the "right to be forgotten", and request the deletion.

From your {{site.data.keyword.cloud_notm}} account, submit a support ticket to `gdprcreq@us.ibm.com`. After you open a support ticket, you will shortly receive an email notification for the ticket. Follow the instructions in the email for further communication on the issue.
